<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade dextrose glucose market can be categorized into two types: API grade and excipient grade. API grade refers to dextrose glucose that is used as an active pharmaceutical ingredient in various drug formulations. It undergoes stringent quality standards and is primarily used for its therapeutic effects. On the other hand, excipient grade dextrose glucose is used as an inactive ingredient in pharmaceutical products. It serves as a filler, binder, or stabilizer, ensuring the proper formulation and integrity of the drug. Both types play a crucial role in pharmaceutical manufacturing, albeit with different functionalities.</p></p>
