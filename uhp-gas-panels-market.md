<p><p>UHP (Ultra High Purity) Gas Panels market includes three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual gas panels require manual intervention for regulating gas flow and pressure, typically with the use of valves and gauges. Semi-automatic gas panels involve a combination of manual control and automated components, such as pre-set pressure regulators. Fully automatic gas panels use advanced control systems to automatically regulate gas flow and pressure without human intervention, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of errors. Each type offers varying degrees of control and automation for gas regulation in different applications.</p></p>
