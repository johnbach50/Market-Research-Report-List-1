<p><p>Network devices that support Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) are available in the market in two main types: access points and routers. Access points are devices that create and extend wireless networks, allowing devices to connect to the internet wirelessly. They are commonly used in commercial spaces like offices, hotels, and public areas. On the other hand, routers are devices that connect multiple devices to a network, enabling data packets to be transferred between devices within a network. Routers are typically used in homes and small businesses for internet connectivity and local network management. Both access points and routers supporting Wi-Fi 6 offer faster speeds, higher capacity, improved efficiency, and better performance compared to previous Wi-Fi standards.</p></p>
