<p><ul><li>Endoparasiticides</li><li>Ectoparasiticides</li><li>Endectocides</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Animal insecticide market types can be classified into three categories: endoparasiticides, ectoparasiticides, and endectocides. Endoparasiticides are used to eliminate internal parasites in animals. Ectoparasiticides help control external parasites, such as ticks and fleas. Endectocides, on the other hand, have a dual action as they target both internal and external parasites. These insecticide types play a crucial role in protecting animals from various harmful parasites, ensuring their health and well-being.</p></p>
