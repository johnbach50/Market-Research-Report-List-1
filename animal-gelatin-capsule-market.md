<p><p>The animal gelatin capsule market is categorized into three types: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin capsules are derived from cattle, porcine from pigs, and piscine from fish collagen. These capsules are widely used in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries. Bovine gelatin capsules are preferred for their stability and compatibility with a wide range of ingredients. Porcine gelatin capsules are known for their better moisture resistance and elasticity. Piscine gelatin capsules are gaining popularity due to their halal and kosher certifications, making them suitable for religious and cultural preferences.  Overall, each market type offers distinct advantages and applications based on their specific animal source.</p></p>
