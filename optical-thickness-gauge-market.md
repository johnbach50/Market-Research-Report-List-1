<p><p>Optical thickness gauges are used to measure the thickness of transparent and translucent materials. The market includes two main types based on the light source used. The first type uses a halogen light source, which produces a wide spectrum of light for accurate measurements. The second type uses an infrared laser diode (LD), which emits narrow wavelength light for precise thickness determination. Both types offer reliable and efficient measurement solutions for various industries, such as glass manufacturing, electronics, and automotive, ensuring quality control and product consistency.</p></p>
