<p><p>Nonfat dry milk powder is available in different types based on the heating process used during production. Low heat nonfat dry milk powder is produced at a lower temperature, which helps preserve its nutritional value and flavor. Medium heat nonfat dry milk powder undergoes moderate heating, balancing nutritional value with increased shelf life. High heat nonfat dry milk powder is heated at a higher temperature, resulting in a longer shelf life but reduced nutritional value. These variations cater to different consumer needs, allowing them to choose based on desired nutritional content and storage duration.</p></p>
