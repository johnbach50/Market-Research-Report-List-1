<p><p>The Ultra Low Power Voltage Detector market consists of three main types: N-ch, P-ch, and CMOS. N-ch refers to N-channel transistors, which are mainly used in low voltage applications and offer high-speed performance. P-ch stands for P-channel transistors, which are used in low voltage circuits and can provide efficient power control. CMOS, or complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor, technology combines the N-ch and P-ch transistors, offering advantages such as low power consumption and high noise immunity. Overall, these types of detectors cater to different requirements in terms of voltage detection and power efficiency.</p></p>
