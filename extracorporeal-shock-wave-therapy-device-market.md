<p><p>Extracorporeal Shock Wave Therapy (ESWT) devices are used for non-invasive treatment of various musculoskeletal conditions. Table-top/Portable ESWT devices are compact and easy to transport, making them suitable for clinics with limited space or for mobile medical services. On the other hand, Hand-push Type ESWT devices require manual movement by the operator, allowing for more precise targeting of the shock waves to specific areas of the body. Both types offer effective ESWT treatment options, catering to different medical settings and patient needs.</p></p>
