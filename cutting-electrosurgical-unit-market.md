<p><strong>The Cutting Electrosurgical Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Electrosurgery</li><li>Monopolar Electrosurgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrosurgical units are used in various medical procedures for cutting and coagulation. Bipolar electrosurgery involves the use of two electrode tips to deliver the electrical current, making it precise and controlled for delicate surgeries. Monopolar electrosurgery uses a single electrode tip and a return electrode pad on the patient's body to complete the electrical circuit, allowing for deeper tissue penetration. Both types of electrosurgery have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the medical procedure.</p></p>
