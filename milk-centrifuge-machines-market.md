<p><p>Milk centrifuge machines come in different types, including hand-operated and motor-operated models. Hand centrifuge machines are manually operated, requiring physical effort to spin the milk at high speeds and separate the different components. On the other hand, motor-operated centrifuge machines have an electric motor that spins the milk, eliminating the need for manual effort. Both types of machines are used in the milk industry for separating cream from milk and obtaining various milk fractions for different dairy products.</p></p>
