There are two types of hyaluronic acid fillers in the market based on their structure and composition. Single-phase fillers have a uniform consistency throughout, while duplex fillers consist of different layers or phases with varying properties. Single-phase fillers are often used for superficial wrinkles, while duplex fillers are more suitable for deeper wrinkles and facial contouring. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
