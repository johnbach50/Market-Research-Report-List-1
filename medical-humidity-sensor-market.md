<p><p>The medical humidity sensor market consists of two main types: relative humidity sensor and absolute humidity sensor. A relative humidity sensor measures the amount of moisture in the air relative to the maximum amount it can hold at a given temperature. It is commonly used in hospitals to monitor patient comfort and prevent the growth of bacteria. On the other hand, an absolute humidity sensor measures the exact amount of water vapor present in the air regardless of temperature. This type of sensor is often employed in medical research studies to analyze the effect of specific humidity levels on different health conditions.</p></p>
