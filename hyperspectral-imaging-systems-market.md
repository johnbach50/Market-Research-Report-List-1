<p><p>Hyperspectral imaging systems are used to capture and analyze the spectral information of objects. The market can be categorized into four types based on the wavelength range of imaging: Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR), Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). VNIR systems focus on the visible and near-infrared spectrum, SWIR systems capture the short wave infrared region, MWIR systems analyze the medium wave infrared spectrum, and LWIR systems operate in the long wave infrared range. Each type has its unique applications and technological requirements.</p></p>
