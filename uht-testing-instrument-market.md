<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>UHT testing instruments can be categorized into two main types: direct UHT testing and indirect UHT testing. Direct UHT testing instruments are used to directly test the quality and safety of UHT treated products, such as dairy and beverages. They measure parameters like temperature, pressure, and flow during the UHT process. On the other hand, indirect UHT testing instruments evaluate the overall quality of the final UHT treated products through various tests, such as sensory analysis, microbial count, and chemical analysis. Both types of instruments play a crucial role in ensuring the effectiveness and safety of UHT treatment.</p></p>
