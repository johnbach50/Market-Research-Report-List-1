<p><p>Ball Bearing Rod End Market includes two types: Internal Threads and External Threads. In Internal Threads, the ball bearing rod end has threads on the inside, allowing for connection with an external threaded component. This type is commonly used in applications where the rod end needs to be connected to a stationary object. On the other hand, External Threads involve threads on the outside of the ball bearing rod end, which enables connection with an internally threaded component. This type is often utilized in applications where the rod end needs to be attached to a moving object.</p></p>
