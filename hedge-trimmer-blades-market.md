<p><p>The hedge trimmer blades market can be categorized into two types: single blade and double blade. Single blade trimmers consist of a single long blade that moves back and forth to cut the hedges. They are generally more affordable but may require more time and effort to achieve desired results. Double blade trimmers, on the other hand, have two blades that move in opposite directions, allowing for quicker and more efficient cutting. Double blade trimmers are often considered more professional and suitable for larger hedge trimming tasks.</p></p>
