<p><p>The Laboratory Faucets Market offers three types of faucets: Single-Hole, Two-Hole, and Three-Hole. Single-Hole faucets are designed to fit into a single sink hole, typically used in smaller laboratories. Two-Hole faucets have separate handles and are suitable for larger sinks, offering hot and cold water control. Three-Hole faucets have two handles and an additional hole for a sprayer or soap dispenser. These are commonly used in more complex laboratory settings where multiple functions are required. Overall, the different types cater to various laboratory needs and sink sizes.</p></p>
