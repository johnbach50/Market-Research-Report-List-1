<p><p>The infrared flame detectors market consists of two main types: single channel detectors and multi-channel detectors. Single channel detectors rely on a single sensor to detect flames within a specific wavelength range. They are cost-effective and widely used in small-scale applications. On the other hand, multi-channel detectors contain multiple sensors that detect flames across a broader range of wavelengths. They offer a higher level of accuracy, are more suitable for large-scale applications, and provide advanced features such as false alarm rejection and better detection range.</p></p>
