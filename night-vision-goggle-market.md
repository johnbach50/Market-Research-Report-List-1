<p><p>The night vision goggle market consists primarily of two types: LLL (low-light-level) night vision goggles and infrared night vision goggles. LLL night vision goggles amplify the available ambient light to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, even in moonlight or starlight. They are typically used for activities like hunting, camping, or wildlife observation. On the other hand, infrared night vision goggles use infrared technology to capture and detect thermal radiation, enabling vision in complete darkness. This makes them commonly used by law enforcement agencies, military personnel, and security professionals for surveillance and tactical operations.</p></p>
