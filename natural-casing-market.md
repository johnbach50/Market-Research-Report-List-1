<p><ul><li>Hog Casing</li><li>Beef Casing</li><li>Sheep Casing</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The natural casing market primarily consists of four types: hog casing, beef casing, sheep casing, and others. Hog casing, made from pig intestines, is widely used in the production of sausages due to its elasticity and sturdy texture. Beef casing, derived from beef intestines, is typically used for larger diameter sausages such as hot dogs. Sheep casing, sourced from sheep intestines, is commonly utilized for smaller sausage varieties. Lastly, the "others" category includes natural casings made from other animals like goats, horses, or poultry. These different types of casings cater to diverse sausage production needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
