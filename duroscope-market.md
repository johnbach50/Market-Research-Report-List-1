<p><p>Duroscope is a term used to classify different types of testing methods for measuring the hardness of materials. The market for Duroscope encompasses various types, including Brinell Duroscope, Vickers Duroscope, Rockwell Duroscope, and others. Brinell Duroscope uses a metal ball to measure hardness, Vickers Duroscope employs a square-based diamond pyramid for testing, and Rockwell Duroscope utilizes a diamond cone or steel ball. These methods differ in their load, indenter shape, and measurement calculation, but all serve the purpose of assessing material hardness in different industrial applications.</p></p>
