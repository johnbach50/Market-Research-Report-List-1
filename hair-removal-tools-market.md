<p><p>Hair removal tools market includes various types of products such as epilators, laser devices, and IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) devices. Epilators are handheld devices that mechanically pull out multiple hair strands at the root, resulting in longer-lasting hair removal. Laser hair removal devices emit concentrated beams of light that target hair follicles, inhibiting their growth. IPL devices use broad-spectrum light to target melanin in the hair follicles, impairing their ability to regrow. These tools offer different hair removal methods, providing consumers with a range of options to suit their preferences and needs.</p></p>
