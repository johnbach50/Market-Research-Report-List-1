<p><p>Elevated dog bowls come in two main market types - adjustable height and unadjustable height. Adjustable height bowls allow pet owners to customize the height of the bowl according to their dog's size or breed. This feature ensures that dogs of different heights can comfortably eat or drink without straining their neck or back. On the other hand, unadjustable height bowls have a fixed height, often designed for specific dog sizes. These bowls offer a convenient and ergonomic feeding solution, but may not be suitable for dogs with special height requirements.</p></p>
