<p><p>The medical heating blanket market is segmented into three types: full body, lower body, and upper body. Full body heating blankets cover the entire body and provide overall temperature regulation. Lower body heating blankets are designed specifically for the lower half of the body, including legs and feet, and help to maintain warmth in these areas. Upper body heating blankets, on the other hand, focus on the upper half of the body, including chest and shoulders, and provide targeted heating for that part. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences in medical settings.</p></p>
