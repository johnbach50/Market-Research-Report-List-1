<p><p>The Micro Lenses market is comprised of various types of lenses including N-BK7, ZnS, Fused Silica, Silicon, and Others. N-BK7 lenses are made from optical glass with high transmission and low absorption. ZnS lenses are mostly used in infrared applications due to their low absorption in the IR spectra. Fused Silica lenses offer excellent thermal stability and low thermal expansion. Silicon lenses have a wide range of applications due to their high refractive index. Lastly, the "Others" category includes lenses made from different materials such as sapphire, germanium, and calcium fluoride.</p></p>
