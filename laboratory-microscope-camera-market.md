<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laboratory Microscope Camera Market is mainly categorized into two types: CCD (Charge Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras use a specialized sensor technology to convert light into electrical signals, offering high image quality and sensitivity. On the other hand, CMOS cameras utilize a different sensor technology which provides faster imaging speed, lower power consumption, and lower cost. Both types of cameras are widely used in laboratory settings to capture and analyze microscopic images for research, diagnosis, and documentation purposes.</p></p>
