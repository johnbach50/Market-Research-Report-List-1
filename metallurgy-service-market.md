<p><p>Metallurgy service market types encompass various techniques used in metal extraction and purification. Sample preparation involves preparing metal samples for analysis. Comminution refers to grinding and crushing ores to facilitate further processing. Gravity concentration involves using the force of gravity to separate heavy minerals from lighter ones. Flotation utilizes chemical reagents to separate valuable minerals from gangue minerals. Magnetic separation utilizes magnetic properties to separate magnetic materials from non-magnetic ones. Hydrometallurgy involves using aqueous solutions to extract and purify metals from ores. These methods play vital roles in the metallurgical industry.</p></p>
