<p><strong>The Coordinate Measuring Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bridge Machinery</li><li>Horizontal Machinery</li><li>Articulated-Arm Machinery</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Coordinate Measuring Machinery market comprises various types of machines used for precise measurement of objects. Bridge Machinery refers to CMMs with a stationary bridge-like structure, allowing movement of the measuring probe along the X and Y axes. Horizontal Machinery involves a horizontal arm that moves along the X axis while the measuring probe moves along the Z axis. Articulated-Arm Machinery utilizes a rotating arm with flexible joints for measuring objects. The "Others" category includes alternative CMM designs such as laser trackers or optical CMMs. These machines aid in accurate dimensional measurements for quality control purposes in industries like manufacturing and automotive.</p></p>
