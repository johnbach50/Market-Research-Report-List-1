<p><p>Metal chelates are compounds that consist of a metal ion attached to a chelating agent. In the market, metal chelates are categorized into three types based on their role in plant nutrition. Essential nutrient metal chelates provide necessary elements like iron and manganese that plants require for growth and development. Auxiliary nutrient metal chelates enhance the effectiveness of fertilizers by improving their uptake and utilization by plants. Micronutrient metal chelates supply trace elements like zinc and copper that are needed in small quantities but are crucial for various plant functions.</p></p>
