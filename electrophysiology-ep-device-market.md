<p><strong>The Electrophysiology (EP) Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EP Ablation Catheters</li><li>EP Diagnostic Catheters</li><li>EP Mapping/Recording System</li><li>LAA</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrophysiology (EP) device market consists of various types of devices used for diagnosing and treating abnormal heart rhythms. EP Ablation Catheters are used to deliver energy to the heart tissue to eliminate abnormal electrical signals. EP Diagnostic Catheters are used to measure electrical signals within the heart to diagnose and locate the source of arrhythmias. EP Mapping/Recording Systems are used to create detailed maps of the heart's electrical activity. Left Atrial Appendage (LAA) devices are used to close off a small pouch in the heart to reduce the risk of stroke. Other devices in the market include cardiac monitors, implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs), and pacemakers.</p></p>
