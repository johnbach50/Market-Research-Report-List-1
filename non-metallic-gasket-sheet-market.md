<p><p>The Non-Metallic Gasket Sheet market can be categorized into various types, including Compressed Fiber, Non-Asbestos, PTFE, and Graphite. Compressed fiber gaskets are made from organic or inorganic fibers bonded with synthetic rubber or other binders. Non-asbestos gaskets are comprised of materials that do not contain asbestos, such as aramid fibers or glass fibers. PTFE gaskets are made from Polytetrafluoroethylene and offer excellent chemical resistance. Graphite gaskets are manufactured from flexible graphite sheets and are known for their high temperature resistance and sealing properties.</p></p>
