<p><p>Quality Management System (QMS) Software is available in two primary market types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise QMS software refers to a locally installed system, where the software and data reside on the organization's own servers. This type gives organizations full control over their data and customization options but requires maintenance and upgrades. In contrast, cloud-based QMS software operates on remote servers and accessed via the internet. This scalable and flexible type offers easier implementation, automatic updates, and lower infrastructure costs, making it popular among organizations with limited IT resources.</p></p>
