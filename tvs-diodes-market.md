<p><p>TVS (Transient Voltage Suppression) diodes are electronic components used to protect sensitive devices from voltage spikes and transients. The TVS Diodes market typically includes two types: Uni-polar TVS and Bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against voltage spikes occurring in one direction, commonly used in low voltage applications. On the other hand, Bi-polar TVS diodes safeguard against voltage spikes occurring in both directions, making them suitable for high voltage applications. Both types of TVS diodes play a crucial role in protecting electronic devices from damage caused by sudden voltage surges.</p></p>
