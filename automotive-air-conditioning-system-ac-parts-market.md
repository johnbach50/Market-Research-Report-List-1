<p><p>The automotive air conditioning system (A/C) parts market is divided into two main types: Orifice Tube System Type A/C and Expansion Valve System Type A/C. In the Orifice Tube System, the orifice tube controls the flow of refrigerant and removes moisture from the system. This system is commonly found in older vehicles. On the other hand, the Expansion Valve System uses an expansion valve to regulate the flow of refrigerant, providing better control over cooling. This system is typically used in newer vehicles, offering improved efficiency and performance.</p></p>
