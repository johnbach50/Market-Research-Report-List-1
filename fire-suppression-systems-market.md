<p><p>The fire suppression systems market consists of various types, including chemical, gaseous, water, and foam. Chemical fire suppression systems use chemicals to extinguish fires by interrupting the chemical reaction of the fire. Gaseous systems, on the other hand, release gases into the protected area to displace oxygen and suppress the fire. Water-based systems use water as a primary agent to cool and extinguish fires. Foam fire suppression systems create a foam blanket over the fire, cutting off the oxygen supply and smothering the flames.</p></p>
