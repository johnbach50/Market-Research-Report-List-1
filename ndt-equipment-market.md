<p><p>NDT Equipment Market Types refers to the different methods used for non-destructive testing. Magnetic Particle Testing involves using a magnetic field to detect surface and near-surface defects in materials. Visual Testing involves visual inspections to identify surface irregularities. Liquid Penetrant Testing uses a liquid dye to reveal surface cracks and discontinuities. Radiographic Testing uses X-rays to examine internal structures of objects. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect defects and measure material thickness. Lastly, "Others" category includes additional NDT methods like eddy current testing and acoustic emission testing.</p></p>
