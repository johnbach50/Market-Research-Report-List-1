<p><p>The prostate cancer devices market includes several types of treatment options. Robotic surgery devices utilize robotic-assisted technology to perform surgical procedures with precision. HIFU devices employ high-intensity focused ultrasound to heat and destroy cancer cells. Cryotherapy devices use extreme cold to freeze and eliminate cancer cells. These various devices aim to provide effective and minimally invasive treatment options for prostate cancer patients.</p></p>
