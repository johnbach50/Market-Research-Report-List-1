This has further contributed to the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the growing popularity of video streaming services and the rising consumer preference for high-quality visuals are driving the demand for video splitters in the consumer electronics sector. The increasing use of video splitters in gaming consoles, home theaters, and virtual reality devices is expected to fuel market growth.</p><p>The video splitters market is witnessing several trends that are expected to shape its growth in the coming years. One notable trend is the integration of video splitters with advanced features such as 4K resolution support, HDCP (High-bandwidth Digital Content Protection), and remote control capabilities. This allows users to transmit high-resolution videos and ensures secure content transmission.</p><p>Moreover, the advent of wireless video splitters is gaining traction in the market. These wireless splitters eliminate the need for physical cables, providing flexibility and ease of installation.
