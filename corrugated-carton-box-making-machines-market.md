<p><p>Corrugated carton box making machines are categorized into two types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention during the manufacturing process, such as feeding the cardboard sheets or gluing the flaps. These machines are suitable for small-scale production or operations with limited resources. On the other hand, fully-automatic machines operate without manual assistance throughout the entire manufacturing process. They are more efficient and suitable for large-scale production, providing increased productivity and reduced labor costs.</p></p>
