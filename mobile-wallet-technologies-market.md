<p><p>Mobile wallet technologies can be categorized into three main types based on how transactions are conducted: proximity, remote, and digital only. Proximity wallets use Near Field Communication (NFC) to facilitate contactless payments at physical points of sale. Remote wallets enable transactions through QR codes or text-based/Short Message Service (SMS) methods, allowing users to make purchases remotely. Digital only wallets are app-based and solely operate online, eliminating the need for physical cards. These market types cater to different consumer preferences and offer convenient and secure payment options in today's digital era.</p></p>
