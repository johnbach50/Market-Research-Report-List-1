<p><p>Conflict checking software is a type of legal technology that enables law firms and legal departments to identify potential conflicts of interest before taking on new clients or matters. Conflict checking software automates the process of cross-referencing client information against various databases to quickly identify any conflicts.</p><p>The conflict checking software market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years. The increasing adoption of technology in the legal sector, coupled with the rising number of legal cases and the growing demand for efficient management of conflicts of interest, are the primary factors driving market growth.
