<p><p>Ladle drainage sand is commonly used in the steel industry for purifying molten metal during the casting process. There are different types of ladle drainage sand available, including magnesium sand, zirconium sand, and chromite sand. Magnesium sand is known for its high thermal stability, zirconium sand for its resistance to thermal shock, and chromite sand for its excellent heat transfer properties. Each type of ladle drainage sand has specific applications and advantages, making them suitable for various metal casting processes.</p></p>
