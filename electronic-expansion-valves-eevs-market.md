There are two main market types of EEVs: Electromagnetic EEVs and Electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs utilize electromagnetic forces to control the valve opening based on feedback from sensors, while Electric EEVs use electric signals to regulate the valve opening. These market types offer different advantages and are chosen based on specific application requirements, such as precision control, energy efficiency, and system size.</p></p>
