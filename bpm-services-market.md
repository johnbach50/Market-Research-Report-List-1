<p><p>BPM services refer to business process management services that help organizations streamline and improve their business processes. The market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based BPM services involve the use of software hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage processes online. On-premise BPM services, on the other hand, are installed and operated within the organization's own infrastructure. While cloud-based solutions offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, on-premise solutions offer better control and data security. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
