<p><p>The marine screw propeller market consists of different types of propulsion systems used in marine vessels. Propellers are traditional screw-like devices that generate thrust by rotating in the water. They are the most widely used type of propulsion system. Thrusters, on the other hand, are compact propellers that are specifically designed for maneuvering and positioning of vessels. They provide lateral thrust and are commonly used in dynamic positioning systems. Lastly, the "others" category includes alternative propulsion systems like water jets or impellers, which function by forcing water out of a nozzle to create propulsion.</p></p>
