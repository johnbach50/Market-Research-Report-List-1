<p><ul><li>Spin Column Method</li><li>Magnetic Bead Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Nucleic Acid Extraction Instrument market includes different types of extraction methods. The Spin Column Method involves passing the sample through a column, where nucleic acids selectively bind to a membrane, while impurities are washed away. The Magnetic Bead Method utilizes magnetic beads that bind to nucleic acids, allowing for easy separation and purification from other components. These methods are used in various applications for isolating nucleic acids and are important tools in molecular biology research, genetic testing, and diagnostics.</p></p>
