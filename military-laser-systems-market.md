<p><p>The military laser systems market encompasses various types of lasers: fiber lasers, chemical lasers, CO2 lasers, and semiconductor lasers. Fiber lasers make use of a fiber-optic cable to amplify laser beams, providing high power and efficiency. Chemical lasers rely on a chemical reaction to generate laser light, offering excellent beam quality. CO2 lasers employ a gas mixture to produce mid-infrared laser beams, suitable for precise targeting. Semiconductor lasers employ lasers made of semiconductor materials and are widely used in target acquisition and guidance systems due to their compact size and low power consumption.</p></p>
