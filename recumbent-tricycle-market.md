<p><p>The recumbent tricycle market is divided into two main types: Tadpole Style and Delta Style. Tadpole Style tricycles have two wheels in the front and one in the back, resembling the shape of a tadpole. They offer improved stability and maneuverability, making them suitable for recreational purposes. Delta Style tricycles, on the other hand, have two wheels in the back and one in the front, resembling the shape of the Greek letter delta. They tend to have a larger cargo capacity and are often used for transportation or touring purposes.</p></p>
