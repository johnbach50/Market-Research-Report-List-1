In-vitro fertilization (IVF): This involves the fertilization of an egg and sperm outside the body, with subsequent embryo implantation into the uterus.</p><p>2. Artificial Insemination-Intrauterine Insemination (AI-IUI): Sperm is directly inserted into the woman's uterus during ovulation.</p><p>3. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER): Frozen embryos from a previous IVF cycle are thawed and implanted into the uterus.</p><p>4. Other market: This includes various techniques like gamete intrafallopian transfer (GIFT), zygote intrafallopian transfer (ZIFT), and intracytoplasmic sperm injection (ICSI). These methods facilitate fertilization and implantation by manipulating sperm, eggs, or embryos.</p></p>
