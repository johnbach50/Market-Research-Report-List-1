<p><p>Robotic deburring machines are used to remove unwanted material or burrs from the surface of various objects. The market includes different types of machines such as rotary transfer deburring, high-pressure deburring, ultrasonic deburring, and others. Rotary transfer deburring machines use a rotating carousel to process multiple parts simultaneously, while high-pressure deburring machines employ compressed air or water for burr removal. Ultrasonic deburring machines use high-frequency sound waves to eliminate burrs. Other types in the market refer to any additional deburring machine variants available that don't fall into the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
