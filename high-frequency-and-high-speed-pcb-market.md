<p><p>The high frequency PCB market includes double board and multilayer board segments. Double board refers to a type of PCB that consists of two layers of conducting materials separated by a dielectric layer. It is ideal for circuits with moderate complexity and provides better signal quality. On the other hand, multilayer boards are more complex and consist of multiple layers of conducting materials separated by dielectric layers. They are suitable for high-speed applications and enable better signal integrity, reducing electromagnetic interference. Both segments cater to the demand for high performance and fast signaling in various industries.</p></p>
