Software Market: In this type, the hybrid cloud management software is provided as a service or a subscription-based model by a third-party vendor. The software is accessed and used by the customers over the internet, without the need for any local installation or maintenance on their own infrastructure. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as the software is managed and updated by the vendor.</p><p>2.
