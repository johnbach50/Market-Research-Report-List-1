<p><p>Microwave tempering systems are used to thaw or temper frozen food products rapidly and uniformly. They come in two main types: Tunnel Microwave Tempering Systems and Box Microwave Tempering Systems. Tunnel systems are designed for continuous processing of large quantities of food products on a conveyor belt, making them suitable for industrial-scale production. Box systems, on the other hand, are typically smaller and designed for batch processing in smaller food production facilities or kitchens. Both types use microwave technology to enhance the thawing process and ensure consistent results.</p></p>
