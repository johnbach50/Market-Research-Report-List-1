<p><p>The UV indicator card market consists of different types based on the range of ultraviolet (UV) rays they detect. UVA indicator cards identify UVA rays, which penetrate deep into the skin and can cause premature aging. UVB indicator cards detect UVB rays responsible for sunburns and skin cancer. UVC indicator cards monitor UVC rays used mainly for disinfection purposes. UVD indicator cards assess the total UV radiation, including all three types. Each type serves unique purposes in measuring and monitoring UV exposure levels in various settings like skincare, sun protection, and sterilization applications.</p></p>
