<p><strong>The Femoral Venous Cannulae Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Two-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Femoral Venous Cannulae market consists of two types: single-stage and two-stage. In the single-stage market, the cannula is inserted directly into the femoral vein, providing a simple and straight-forward procedure. On the other hand, the two-stage market involves a two-step process where a catheter is first inserted into the femoral vein and then connected to a separate cannula. This two-stage approach allows for better control and monitoring of blood flow during medical procedures. Both types cater to specific preferences and requirements of medical professionals.</p></p>
