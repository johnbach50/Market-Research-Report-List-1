<p><p>Automatic Weigh Labellers consist of two main types - Semi-automatic and Fully automatic weigh labellers. </p><p>Semi-automatic Weigh Labellers require some manual intervention during the labeling process. Operators need to place the product on the scale and then attach the label manually. These labellers are suitable for small-scale operations and offer flexibility in terms of various product types and sizes.</p><p>On the other hand, Fully automatic Weigh Labellers are more advanced and require minimal manual involvement. These labellers can automate the entire labeling process, including product weighing and label application. They are ideal for high-volume production environments where efficiency and precision are crucial.</p></p>
