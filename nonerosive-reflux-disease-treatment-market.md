<p><p>Nonerosive reflux disease (NERD) is a chronic condition characterized by symptoms of gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) such as heartburn and acid regurgitation, but without evidence of erosions or ulcers in the esophagus. The aim of NERD treatment is to alleviate symptoms, improve patients' quality of life, and prevent complications. The treatment options for NERD include lifestyle modifications, over-the-counter antacids, and prescription medications.</p><p>Lifestyle modifications involve avoiding trigger foods, maintaining a healthy weight, quitting smoking, elevating the head of the bed while sleeping, and eating smaller meals. Over-the-counter antacids like Tums or Rolaids provide short-term relief by neutralizing stomach acid. However, for patients with persistent symptoms, prescription medications like proton pump inhibitors (PPIs) or H2 receptor blockers may be recommended to suppress stomach acid production.</p><p>The Nonerosive Reflux Disease Treatment Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. Several factors are driving this growth, including the increasing prevalence of GERD and NERD worldwide. Additionally, the growing aging population and the rising adoption of sedentary lifestyles and unhealthy diets contribute to the market expansion.
