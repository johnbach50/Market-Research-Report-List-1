<p><p>The engine filter market encompasses three main types: air filters, oil filters, and fuel filters. Air filters are responsible for removing dirt, dust, and other contaminants from the air before it enters the engine. Oil filters, on the other hand, remove impurities from the engine oil, ensuring its cleanliness and protecting the engine components. Lastly, fuel filters are designed to eliminate any particles or impurities present in the fuel, preventing them from reaching the engine and causing potential damage. These filters play crucial roles in maintaining the efficiency and longevity of the engine.</p></p>
