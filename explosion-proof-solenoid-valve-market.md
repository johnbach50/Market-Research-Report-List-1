<p><p>The explosion-proof solenoid valve market comprises three types: two-way solenoid valves, three-way solenoid valves, and four-way solenoid valves. Two-way solenoid valves are designed to control the flow of fluid in two different directions. Three-way solenoid valves have three ports and are commonly used to divert the flow of fluid in different directions. On the other hand, four-way solenoid valves, with four ports, are used to regulate the flow of fluid through different interconnected pipes or systems. These valves are crucial in explosion-proof environments where safety is of utmost importance.</p></p>
