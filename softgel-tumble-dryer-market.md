<p><strong>The Softgel Tumble Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Softgel Tumble Dryer market consists of two main types: Standard Tumble Drying and Continuous Tumble Drying. Standard Tumble Drying involves loading a batch of softgel capsules into the dryer and drying them for a fixed period of time before unloading. This method is suitable for small to medium-sized production volumes. On the other hand, Continuous Tumble Drying is a more advanced process where the softgel capsules are continuously fed into the dryer, dried, and discharged in a continuous flow. This method is ideal for high-volume production as it offers a continuous and streamlined operation.</p></p>
