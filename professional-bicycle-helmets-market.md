<p><p>The professional bicycle helmets market offers two main types: half shell helmets and full face helmets. Half shell helmets are light and comfortable, providing protection to the top and sides of the head without covering the face. They are commonly used in road cycling and mountain biking. On the other hand, full face helmets offer complete coverage with added protection for the chin and face. They are primarily used in downhill mountain biking and extreme cycling disciplines where the risk of face impact is higher.</p></p>
