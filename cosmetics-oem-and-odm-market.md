<p><p>OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) is a market where a company produces cosmetics based on the specifications provided by another brand or company, allowing them to sell the products under their own label. ODM (Original Design Manufacturer), on the other hand, involves a company creating and designing cosmetics products that are then sold to other brands who market them under their own label. Both OEM and ODM markets provide opportunities for companies to expand their product offerings without the need to invest in manufacturing facilities or design capabilities.</p></p>
