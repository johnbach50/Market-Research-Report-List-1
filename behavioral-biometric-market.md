Behavioral biometrics provide a frictionless user experience by continuously authenticating users based on their unique behavioral patterns, eliminating the need for passwords or PINs.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the adoption of behavioral biometrics, as businesses seek contactless and secure authentication solutions.
