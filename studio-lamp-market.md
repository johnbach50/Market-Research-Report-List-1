<p><p>In the studio lamp market, two common types are astigmatism lights and modeling lamps. Astigmatism lights are designed to minimize distorted light patterns, providing a more even and uniform illumination for photography and videography purposes. On the other hand, modeling lamps serve as a guide for photographers to visualize the lighting effects by projecting a continuous light source onto the subject before capturing the shot. Both types play crucial roles in the studio setting, ensuring optimal lighting conditions for professional results.</p></p>
