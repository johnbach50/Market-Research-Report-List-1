<p><p>A plastic blood bag is a sterile, disposable bag used for the collection, storage, transfusion, and transportation of blood and blood components. These bags are primarily used in blood banks, hospitals, and other healthcare facilities.</p><p>The plastic blood bag market has witnessed significant growth in recent years due to the increasing number of blood transfusion procedures, advancements in healthcare infrastructure, and rising prevalence of chronic diseases requiring blood and blood products. The market is further fueled by the growing awareness about blood transfusion safety and the need for infection-free storage and transportation of blood.</p><p>Furthermore, the latest trends in the plastic blood bag market include the increasing adoption of RFID technology for tracking blood bags, the development of blood bags with oxygen absorbers for improved storage, and the introduction of eco-friendly and biodegradable blood bags to reduce environmental impact.
