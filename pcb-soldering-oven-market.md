<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PCB Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Soldering Oven</li><li>Selective Soldering Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering oven market consists of three main types: wave soldering oven, reflow soldering oven, and selective soldering oven. A wave soldering oven is used for mass production and uses a wave of molten solder to solder through-hole components. Reflow soldering ovens are used in surface mount technology and rely on infrared heating to melt solder paste and connect components to PCBs. Selective soldering ovens are employed for precision soldering, targeting specific areas, and are useful for complex circuit boards that require high accuracy and repeatability.</p></p>
