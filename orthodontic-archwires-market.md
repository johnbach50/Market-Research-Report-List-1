<p><p>Orthodontic archwires are crucial components used in orthodontic treatment to align and straighten teeth. These archwires come in different materials, including Beta Titanium, Nickel Titanium, and Stainless Steel. Beta Titanium archwires are known for their flexibility, shape memory, and resistance to deformation. Nickel Titanium archwires offer similar properties but are more resistant to corrosion. On the other hand, Stainless Steel archwires are rigid, exerting greater force on teeth. Each material has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements and treatment plan of the patient.</p></p>
