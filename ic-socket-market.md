Test and Burn-in Sockets aid in testing and burning-in chips prior to final assembly. Dual-in-line Package sockets are used for general-purpose chips. Specialty Sockets are designed for specific chip applications, such as microcontrollers or microprocessors. Each type serves a unique purpose in the IC socket market.</p></p>
