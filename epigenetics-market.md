<p><p>Epigenetics refers to the study of changes in gene expression that do not involve alterations to the genetic code itself. The Epigenetics market comprises various types of technologies used to analyze and understand these changes. DNA methylation involves the addition of a methyl group to the DNA molecule, which affects gene expression. Histone modifications refer to alterations in the histone proteins around which DNA is wrapped, influencing gene activity. Other technologies include chromatin remodeling, non-coding RNA, and post-translational modifications. These technologies enable researchers to unlock the potential of epigenetic modifications in disease diagnosis, treatment, and drug development.</p></p>
