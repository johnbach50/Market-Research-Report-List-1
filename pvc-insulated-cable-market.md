<p><p>The PVC Insulated Cable Market consists of two types: PVC Sheathed Armoured Cable and PVC Sheathed Unarmoured Cable. PVC Sheathed Armoured Cable refers to cables that have an additional layer of armoring made of steel or aluminum, providing extra protection against mechanical damage. These cables are commonly used in areas where there is a higher risk of damage, such as underground or outdoor installations. On the other hand, PVC Sheathed Unarmoured Cable refers to cables without the additional armoring layer, suitable for installations where there is a lower risk of damage.</p></p>
