<p><p>Contract management tools can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based contract management tools are hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and easy integration with other systems. On the other hand, on-premises contract management tools are installed and maintained locally on the organization's servers. They provide more control over data and security but require higher upfront costs for infrastructure and ongoing maintenance. The choice between these two types depends on an organization's specific needs, preferences, and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
