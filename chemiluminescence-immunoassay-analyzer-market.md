<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer market encompasses various types of immunoassay techniques. Immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescent molecules to detect specific targets, while Chemiluminescence employs light emission from a chemical reaction. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) detects molecules through enzyme reactions, while Enzyme Linked Fluorescent Systems combine ELISA with fluorescence. Multiplexed Assay Systems enable simultaneous detection of multiple targets. Lastly, Radioimmunoassay utilizes radioactive markers for target detection. These techniques are used for analyzing various biological samples in fields such as diagnostics, research, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
