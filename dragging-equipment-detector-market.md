<p><p>The Dragging Equipment Detector (DED) market consists of two main types: Stationary Impact Plates and Traditional Paddle Set. Stationary Impact Plates are fixed devices placed on the ground that detect dragging equipment by measuring the impact generated when it passes over. They are cost-effective and easy to install. On the other hand, Traditional Paddle Sets are manually operated devices that have been used for a long time. They require operators to manually place the paddle sets underneath the equipment, and their effectiveness relies on the operator's experience and diligence.</p></p>
