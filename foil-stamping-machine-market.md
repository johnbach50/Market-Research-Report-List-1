<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Foil stamping machines are classified into three types based on the shape of their dies: Flat-flat, round-flat, and round-round. The flat-flat type is characterized by two flat dies that press against each other, suitable for stamping on flat surfaces. The round-flat type uses a flat die combined with a round die, enabling stamping on curved or irregular surfaces. Lastly, the round-round type involves two round dies that press against each other, commonly utilized for stamping on cylindrical objects. These variations cater to different stamping requirements and allow versatility in the foil stamping machine market.</p></p>
