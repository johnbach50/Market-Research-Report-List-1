<p><p>The burn spray market is divided into two types: aerosol and non-aerosol. Aerosol burn sprays come in pressurized containers and dispense the product as a fine mist. They are easy to use and provide a cooling effect on the burned area. Non-aerosol burn sprays, on the other hand, are typically pump or squeeze bottles that dispense the product as a liquid or gel. They may require manual application and provide immediate relief by reducing the burning sensation. Both types of burn sprays are designed to provide first-aid treatment for burns and help promote healing.</p></p>
