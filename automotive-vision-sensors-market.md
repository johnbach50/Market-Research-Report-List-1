<p><strong>The Automotive Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular Vision</li><li>Binocular (stereo) Vision</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Vision Sensors market consists of two main types: Monocular Vision and Binocular (stereo) Vision. Monocular Vision refers to the use of a single camera sensor that captures images and provides depth perception using software algorithms. Binocular Vision, on the other hand, utilizes two camera sensors placed at a specific distance apart to mimic human vision, providing a more accurate and precise depth perception capability. These sensors are used in automotive applications to enhance driver assistance systems, object detection, collision avoidance, and autonomous driving technology.</p></p>
