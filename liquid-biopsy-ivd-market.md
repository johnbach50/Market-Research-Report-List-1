<p><strong>The Liquid Biopsy IVD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Tumor Cells</li><li>Circulating Tumor DNA</li><li>Extracellular Vesicles</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid biopsy IVD market comprises various types, including circulating tumor cells (CTCs), circulating tumor DNA (ctDNA), extracellular vesicles, and others. CTCs are cancer cells that separate from the tumor and enter the bloodstream. ctDNA refers to fragments of DNA shed by tumors into the bloodstream. Extracellular vesicles are small membrane-bound sacs that carry biomolecules released by cancer cells. The "others" category encompasses other types of biomarkers or analytes that can be detected in bodily fluids and used to diagnose or monitor cancer. These different types offer diverse approaches for detecting and analyzing cancer-related biomarkers in liquid biopsies.</p></p>
