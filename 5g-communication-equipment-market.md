<p><p>The 5G communication equipment market encompasses various types of cells based on coverage area. Femtocell is designed for small indoor areas, providing limited coverage for homes or offices. Pico cell expands coverage to larger indoor spaces like shopping malls or airports. Micro cell serves medium-sized outdoor areas, such as urban neighborhoods. Macro cell is used for large outdoor areas like cities, offering wide coverage. These cells differ based on their power and range, catering to different needs in terms of both indoor and outdoor connectivity.</p></p>
