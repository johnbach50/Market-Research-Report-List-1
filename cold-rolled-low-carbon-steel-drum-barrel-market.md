<p><p>The cold rolled low carbon steel drum barrel market consists of two main types: open head steel drums and closed head steel drums. Open head steel drums have a removable lid, allowing for easy access and filling of the drum. These drums are commonly used for storing and shipping liquids and solid materials. On the other hand, closed head steel drums have a fixed lid, providing a secure seal. These drums are often used for hazardous materials storage, as the closed design offers greater protection against spills or leaks.</p></p>
