<p><ul><li>Digital Light Projection</li><li>3D Jet Printing</li><li>Stereo Lithography Apparatus (SLA) 3D Printing</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Direct Laser Metal Sintering (DLMS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental 3D printing devices market includes various types of technologies. Digital light projection uses a light source to cure liquid resin into solid objects layer by layer. 3D jet printing utilizes inkjet printing technology to create dental models. Stereo lithography apparatus (SLA) 3D printing employs a laser to solidify liquid resin. Selective laser sintering (SLS) uses a high-power laser to fuse powdered material together. Direct laser metal sintering (DLMS) enables the production of metal dental restorations using a laser to sinter metal powder.</p></p>
