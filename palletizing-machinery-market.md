<p><p>Palletizing machinery refers to the equipment used for arranging and stacking products onto pallets. There are several types of palletizers available in the market. Traditional palletizers are manual or semi-automatic systems where workers place products onto pallets. Robotic palletizers utilize robots for automated stacking, increasing efficiency and flexibility. Cartesian palletizers use a Cartesian coordinate system for precise and versatile palletizing. Mixed palletizing market involves systems that can create pallets with different products, optimizing space and reducing handling costs. These types cater to different needs and requirements in the palletizing industry.</p></p>
