<p><strong>The Facial Recognition Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2D Facial Recognition</li><li>3D Facial Recognition</li><li>Thermal Face Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facial recognition technology is implemented through different market types based on the techniques used. 2D facial recognition analyzes two-dimensional images of faces to identify individuals, commonly used in applications like access control systems. In contrast, 3D facial recognition captures depth information and creates a three-dimensional model of the face, offering higher accuracy in authentication and is used in applications requiring higher security. Thermal face recognition uses infrared cameras to detect and measure the heat patterns emitted by the face, enabling identification even in low-light or harsh conditions, commonly employed for surveillance purposes.</p></p>
