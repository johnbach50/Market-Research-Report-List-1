<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Active Tactile Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators</li><li>Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Tactile Actuator market consists of different types of actuators, including Eccentric Rotating Mass (ERM) Actuators, Linear Resonant Actuators (LRA), and others. ERM actuators rely on an unbalanced mass to create vibrations, generating tactile feedback. On the other hand, LRA actuators use a spring-mass system to generate vibrations at a resonant frequency for haptic feedback. Lastly, the "others" category includes various types of tactile actuators that do not fall under ERM or LRA, such as piezoelectric actuators or Shape Memory Alloy (SMA) actuators.</p></p>
