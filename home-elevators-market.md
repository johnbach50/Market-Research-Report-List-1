<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The home elevator market comprises different types of elevators. Cable driver elevators operate using steel cables and are suitable for taller buildings. Chain driver elevators use chains to move the elevator car and are commonly used in residential settings. Traction (MRL) elevators combine the features of cable and chain driver elevators and are suitable for both residential and commercial buildings. Hydraulic elevators rely on a hydraulic system to move the elevator car and are commonly used in low-rise buildings. Pneumatic lift elevators use air pressure to move the car vertically and are known for their unique design and energy efficiency.</p></p>
