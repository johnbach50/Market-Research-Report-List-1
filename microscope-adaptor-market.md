<p><p>The microscope adaptor market consists of two main types: wired connection and wireless connection. On the other hand, wireless connection adaptors use wireless technology, such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, to establish a connection between the microscope and the device, eliminating the need for any physical cables. Both types of adaptors provide users with convenient options for connecting their microscopes to devices for data transfer and analysis.</p></p>
