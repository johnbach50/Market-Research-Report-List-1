<p><p>The RFID reader market can be classified into four types: LF (low frequency), HF (high frequency), UHF (ultra-high frequency), and MW (microwave). LF RFID readers operate at a frequency range of 30 kHz to 300 kHz and are commonly used for access control and animal tracking applications. HF RFID readers operate at a frequency range of 3 MHz to 30 MHz and find applications in payment systems and library management. UHF RFID readers work in the range of 300 MHz to 3 GHz and are widely used in inventory tracking and logistics. MW RFID readers operate in the microwave range of 2.4 GHz to 5.8 GHz and are suitable for industrial automation and vehicle tracking.</p></p>
