<p><p>Express Digitizer Market Types refer to different form factors used for digitizing signals in test and measurement applications. PXI (Peripheral Component Interconnect eXtensions for Instrumentation) is a popular format for modular test systems. PCI (Peripheral Component Interconnect) and PCIe (PCI Express) are standard slots for high-speed data acquisition cards. PXIe combines PXI with PCIe technology for faster performance. USB is a common connectivity option for portable digitizers. Each market type offers unique advantages in terms of speed, flexibility, and compatibility with existing systems.</p></p>
