<p><p>The residential air cooler market is divided into two main types: portable air coolers and fixed air coolers. Fixed air coolers, on the other hand, are permanently installed in a specific area or room of a house. They provide whole-house cooling and are more suitable for larger spaces. Both types of air coolers offer effective cooling solutions for residential purposes.</p></p>
