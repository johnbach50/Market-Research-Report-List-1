<p><p>The disposable ECG electrodes market consists of two main types: monitoring ECG electrodes and diagnostic ECG electrodes. Monitoring ECG electrodes are typically used for continuous monitoring of a patient's electrocardiogram during various medical procedures or in healthcare settings. On the other hand, diagnostic ECG electrodes are used specifically for obtaining high-quality and accurate ECG recordings for diagnostic purposes, such as detecting heart abnormality or disease. Both types of electrodes are designed to be used only once and then discarded, ensuring hygiene and minimizing the risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
