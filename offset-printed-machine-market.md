<p><ul><li>Sheet-Fed Offset Printing Press</li><li>Web-Fed Offset Printing Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offset printing machine market consists of two main types: sheet-fed offset printing press and web-fed offset printing press. A sheet-fed offset printing press is designed to print on individual sheets of paper, which are fed into the machine one at a time. On the other hand, a web-fed offset printing press utilizes a continuous roll of paper, known as a web, which is fed through the machine continuously. Both types of machines are used in the printing industry to produce high-quality prints on a large scale.</p></p>
