<p><strong>The Metal Detector in Food Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balanced Coil Systems</li><li>Ferrous-in-Foil Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal detectors in the food market come in different types, such as balanced coil systems and ferrous-in-foil systems. Balanced coil systems use two coils to detect metal contaminants in the food by creating a balanced electromagnetic field. They are efficient in detecting a wide range of metal types. On the other hand, ferrous-in-foil systems are specifically designed to detect ferrous metal contaminants concealed inside aluminum foil packaging. These systems are crucial for ensuring food safety and quality by preventing metal contamination.</p></p>
