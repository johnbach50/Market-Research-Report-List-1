<p><p>The Internet-connected doorbell market consists of two main types: wireless video doorbells and wireless invisible doorbells. Wireless video doorbells include a built-in camera that allows the user to see and communicate with visitors through their smartphone or computer. They provide added security and convenience by enabling remote monitoring and answering the door from anywhere. On the other hand, wireless invisible doorbells do not have a camera but provide a wireless connection to the user's smartphone, alerting them when someone rings the doorbell. These offer a more discreet option for those seeking a simple notification system.</p></p>
