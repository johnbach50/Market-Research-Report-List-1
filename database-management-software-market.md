<p><p>Database Management Software market includes two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises solutions. Cloud-Based database management software is a type where databases and their management tools are hosted on remote servers accessible via the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced maintenance costs through the subscription-based model. On the other hand, On-Premises database management software involves hosting databases on local servers within the organization's infrastructure. It provides greater control over data security and customization but requires higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs and priorities.</p></p>
