Additionally, the rise in healthcare expenditure and the growing emphasis on infection prevention and control further propel market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the sterilization tunnel market is the integration of advanced technologies such as ultraviolet (UV) light, electromagnetic waves, and ozone generators to enhance the sterilization process.
