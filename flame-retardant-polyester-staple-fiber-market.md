<p><p>The Flame Retardant Polyester Staple Fiber market consists of two types: Inherent FRP Staple Fiber and Treated FRP Staple Fiber. Inherent FRP Staple Fiber refers to polyester fibers that are inherently flame retardant due to the inclusion of specific flame retardant additives during the manufacturing process. On the other hand, Treated FRP Staple Fiber refers to polyester fibers that are treated with flame retardant chemicals after the manufacturing process to enhance their flame retardant properties. Both types of fibers are used in various industries where flame resistance is required.</p></p>
