<p><p>Orthostatic hypotension drugs market includes various types such as Midodrine, Northera (droxidopa), Fludrocortisone, and other drugs. Midodrine works by constricting blood vessels to raise blood pressure. Northera increases norepinephrine levels, improving blood pressure and reducing symptoms. Fludrocortisone is a steroid that helps retain salt and fluids in the body, increasing blood volume. Other drugs may include non-pharmacological options like physical maneuvers or lifestyle changes. These drugs aim to alleviate low blood pressure symptoms when standing up, allowing individuals to maintain normal blood pressure levels in an upright position.</p></p>
