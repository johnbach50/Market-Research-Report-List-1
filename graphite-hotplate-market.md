<p><ul><li>With Anticorrosive Coating</li><li>Without Anticorrosive Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite hotplate market types can be divided into two categories: those with anticorrosive coating and those without anticorrosive coating. Hotplates with anticorrosive coating are designed to resist corrosion caused by chemical reactions with substances being heated on the plate. These types of hotplates are ideal for laboratories and other environments where corrosive materials are used. On the other hand, hotplates without anticorrosive coating are more basic and do not offer the same resistance to corrosion. They are typically used in applications where corrosion is not a concern or the hotplate will be replaced frequently.</p></p>
