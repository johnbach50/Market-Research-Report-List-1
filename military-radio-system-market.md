<p><p>The military radio system market is comprised of two main types: Manpack Radios and Vehicular Radios. Manpack Radios are portable and lightweight communication devices that are designed to be carried by soldiers in the field. They provide secure and reliable communication capabilities for individuals or small groups. On the other hand, Vehicular Radios are larger systems that are mounted on military vehicles. They offer enhanced communication range and power, enabling communication between multiple vehicles or with command headquarters. Both types of radios play a crucial role in ensuring effective and efficient military operations.</p></p>
