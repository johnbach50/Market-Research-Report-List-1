<p><strong>The Slotted Carton Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Slotted Carton</li><li>Regular Slotted Carton</li><li>Overlap Slotted Carton</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Slotted cartons are widely used in packaging and shipping due to their versatility. Half slotted cartons have only one set of flaps and are ideal for products of uniform width but varying heights. Regular slotted cartons have flaps on both the top and bottom, suitable for products of different sizes. Overlap slotted cartons have flaps that overlap fully or partially, providing extra strength and protection. These market types cater to diverse packaging needs, ensuring safe transportation of goods across various industries.</p></p>
