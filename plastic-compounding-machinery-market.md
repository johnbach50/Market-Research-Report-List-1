<p><strong>The Plastic Compounding Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Screw Extruders</li><li>Twin-Screw Extruders</li><li>Kneaders & Mixers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic compounding machinery refers to different types of equipment used in the process of compounding plastic materials. Single-screw extruders are machines that use a single rotating screw to melt and mix plastic materials. Twin-screw extruders, on the other hand, utilize two intermeshing screws to achieve better mixing and compounding. Kneaders and mixers are machines that use rotating blades or paddles to blend and mix plastic materials. Each type of machinery has its own advantages and applications in the plastic compounding industry, depending on the desired results and requirements of the process.</p></p>
