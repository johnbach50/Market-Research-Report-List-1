These detectors help in precise diagnosis and treatment planning for various medical conditions, including cancer and cardiovascular diseases. Similarly, in the defense and homeland security sector, CZT detectors play a crucial role in identifying and preventing illicit uses of radiation sources.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in CZT technology, such as the development of pixelated CZT detectors, are driving their adoption across different industries.
