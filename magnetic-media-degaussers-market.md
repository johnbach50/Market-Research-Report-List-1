<p><p>The magnetic media degaussers market comprises three main types: coil degaussers, capacitive discharge degaussers, and permanent magnet degaussers. Coil degaussers use a high-frequency magnetic field generated by a coil to erase data from magnetic media such as hard drives. Capacitive discharge degaussers utilize a strong electrical discharge to create a strong magnetic field and remove data from media quickly. Permanent magnet degaussers use permanent magnets to demagnetize the media and erase data effectively. These degaussers cater to different needs in securely erasing magnetic media.</p></p>
