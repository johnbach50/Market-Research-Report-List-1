<p><p>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Fiber market includes two main types: Staple fiber and Filament Yarn. </p><p>Staple fiber refers to short fibers that are twisted together to create yarns used in textiles, upholstery, and non-woven fabrics. It is known for its durability and wrinkle resistance, making it popular in the apparel industry.</p><p>Filament yarn, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands of fibers that are used in making ropes, nets, and industrial fabrics. It is favored for its high strength and low stretch properties.</p></p>
