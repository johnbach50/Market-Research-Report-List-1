<p><p>The veterinary dental software market consists of two types: 2D imaging and 3D imaging. 2D imaging refers to the capture of two-dimensional images of the animal's oral cavity, allowing veterinarians to observe and diagnose dental issues. On the other hand, 3D imaging involves the collection of three-dimensional images, providing a more comprehensive and detailed view of the animal's dental structure. Both types of imaging technology are essential in veterinary dentistry as they assist in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning for dental health issues in animals, improving overall patient care.</p></p>
