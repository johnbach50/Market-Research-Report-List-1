<p><p>The alarm buzzer market consists of two main types: active buzzer and passive buzzer. Active buzzers generate sound on their own when provided with a power supply, making them simple to use. They often feature built-in oscillators for generating different tones. On the other hand, passive buzzers are driven by an external signal and require an oscillating input to produce sound. They offer more control over the generated tone and are commonly used in applications where specific sounds are necessary, such as alarm systems.</p></p>
