<p><p>Differential pressure meters are used to measure the pressure difference between two points in a system. There are three main types: piston style, diaphragm type, and bellows differential type. </p><p>Piston style meters use a piston-moving mechanism to create the pressure difference and measure it. These meters are highly accurate but can be bulky and expensive. </p><p>Diaphragm type meters use a flexible diaphragm to measure the pressure difference. They are more compact, cost-effective, and suitable for low-pressure applications. </p><p>Bellows differential type meters utilize a bellows mechanism to measure the pressure difference. They offer high sensitivity and accuracy, making them ideal for demanding applications but can be expensive to manufacture.</p></p>
