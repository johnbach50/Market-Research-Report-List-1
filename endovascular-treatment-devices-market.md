<p><strong>The Endovascular Treatment Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endovascular Aortic Repair (EVAR)</li><li>Thoracic Endovascular Aortic/aneurysm Repair (TEVAR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endovascular treatment devices are utilized in minimally invasive procedures to repair aortic aneurysms. Endovascular Aortic Repair (EVAR) specifically focuses on repairing abdominal aortic aneurysms, while Thoracic Endovascular Aortic/aneurysm Repair (TEVAR) targets thoracic aortic aneurysms. Both procedures involve the insertion of a stent graft through a small incision in the groin, reducing the risk and recovery time associated with traditional open surgery. These techniques have become increasingly popular due to their lower invasiveness and potential for shorter hospital stays.</p></p>
