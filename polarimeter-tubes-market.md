<p><p>Polarimeter tubes are used to measure the rotation of polarized light by a substance. Two types of polarimeter tubes are long and short tubes. Long tubes have a greater length and are ideal for samples with high optical activity or when high accuracy is required. Short tubes have a smaller length and are suitable for samples with low optical activity or when rapid measurements are needed. Both types serve the purpose of measuring the angle of rotation of polarized light, but they differ in length and application.</p></p>
