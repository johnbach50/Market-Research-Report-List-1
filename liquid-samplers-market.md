<p><p>Liquid samplers are devices used for the collection of samples from liquids. Single point samplers collect a sample from a specific point in the liquid, allowing for precise and targeted sampling. Cross-section sampling, on the other hand, collects samples from different points across the entire cross-section of the liquid, providing a representative sample of the entire volume. Both types of samplers are used in various industries for quality control, research, and analysis purposes, ensuring accurate and reliable sampling for further examination.</p></p>
