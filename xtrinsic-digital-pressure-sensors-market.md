<p><p>Xtrinsic Digital Pressure Sensors are classified into three main types: Relative Digital Pressure Sensors, Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors, and Differential Digital Pressure Sensors. Relative Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to the atmospheric pressure. Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a full vacuum. Differential Digital Pressure Sensors measure the difference between two different pressures. These sensors are widely used in various applications such as automotive, healthcare, industrial, and aerospace industries, to accurately measure and monitor pressure levels for optimal performance and safety.</p></p>
