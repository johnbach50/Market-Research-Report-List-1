<p><strong>The Roller Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Row</li><li>Double-Row</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Roller Bearings market can be categorized into Single-Row and Double-Row types based on the number of rows of rollers in the bearing. Single-Row bearings have a single set of rollers, providing radial load support, while Double-Row bearings have two sets of rollers, offering both radial and axial load support. Single-Row bearings are commonly used in applications with lighter loads, while Double-Row bearings are preferred for heavier loads and applications requiring increased radial and axial load capacity.</p></p>
