<p><p>IQF stands for Individual Quick Freezing, which is a technique used for preserving the texture, color, and nutritional value of fruits. This freezing technique helps to maintain the natural characteristics of fruits, such as taste, texture, and aroma.</p><p>The IQF fruits market has been witnessing significant growth in recent years due to the increasing demand for convenient and healthy food products. The growing preference for frozen fruits over canned or dried fruits, owing to their longer shelf life and better sensory qualities, is driving the market growth. Moreover, the rising awareness among consumers regarding the health benefits of IQF fruits, such as high nutritional value, vitamin content, and antioxidants, is further fueling the market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the adoption of IQF technology by food processing companies for preserving seasonal fruits throughout the year is contributing to the market growth. IQF fruits find extensive applications in various industries, including bakery and confectionery, beverages, dairy products, desserts, and smoothies, further boosting the market demand.</p><p>In terms of market trends, there is a growing demand for organic and natural IQF fruits, as consumers are increasingly inclined towards clean label and chemical-free food products.
