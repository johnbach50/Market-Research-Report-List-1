Source Drive ICs are responsible for controlling the electrical current flowing through each pixel of the display panel, ensuring proper color and brightness. On the other hand, Gate Drive ICs control the timing and voltage levels required to update and refresh the display panel. Both types of ICs play crucial roles in ensuring the optimal performance of small size panel displays.</p></p>
