Fully-automatic guns, on the other hand, continuously spray paint when the trigger is pulled, making large-scale painting projects efficient. Manual guns rely on the user to manually release paint by manipulating the gun's controls. Each type caters to different painting needs, providing options for users with varying preferences and requirements.</p></p>
