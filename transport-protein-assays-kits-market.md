<p><p>Transport protein assays kits are used for studying the function of transport proteins. The market for these kits can be categorized into three types: efflux transporter kits, uptake transporter kits, and consumables. Efflux transporter kits are designed to measure the activity of proteins that remove substances from a cell, while uptake transporter kits measure the activity of proteins that bring substances into a cell. Consumables include reagents, plates, and other materials necessary for conducting the assays. These kits and consumables are essential tools for researchers in drug discovery and development.</p></p>
