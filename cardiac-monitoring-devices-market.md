<p><ul><li>ECG Method</li><li>Arterial Pressure Method</li><li>Photoelectric Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiac monitoring devices involve various methods for monitoring the functioning of the heart. The ECG method records the electrical activity of the heart to detect abnormalities. The arterial pressure method measures the pressure within the arteries to monitor blood flow and heart rate. The photoelectric method utilizes light absorption and reflection to monitor blood oxygen levels and heart rate. These methods enable healthcare professionals to diagnose and manage cardiac conditions by providing accurate and real-time data about the heart's performance.</p></p>
