<p><p>Radiation protection windows are specialized windows used in various applications such as medical, nuclear power plants, and research facilities to protect workers and the environment from the harmful effects of radiation exposure. The market growth can be attributed to the increasing applications of radiation protection windows across various industries, particularly in the medical sector. The rising adoption of radiation therapy for cancer treatment and the growing number of diagnostic imaging procedures are driving the demand for radiation protection windows in hospitals and clinics.</p><p>Furthermore, the increased focus on nuclear power generation as a clean energy source is also contributing to the market growth. Nuclear power plants require radiation protection windows in control rooms, observation areas, and maintenance facilities to ensure the safety of workers and prevent radiation leaks.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology are fueling the market growth by introducing innovative radiation protection window materials that offer enhanced protection and durability. These regulations mandate the use of radiation shielding materials, including radiation protection windows, in facilities involving the use of ionizing radiation.</p><p>In conclusion, the radiation protection window market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing applications of radiation protection windows in medical, nuclear power plants, and research facilities, along with technological advancements and stringent regulations, are the key factors driving market growth.</p></p>
