<p><strong>The Gene Sequencer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene sequencer market types can be categorized into two main technologies: plate electrophoresis and capillary electrophoresis. In plate electrophoresis, samples are loaded onto a gel within a plate, and an electric field is applied to separate the DNA fragments based on size. This method is suitable for large-scale processing but requires more time and labor. On the other hand, capillary electrophoresis utilizes capillary tubes to separate DNA fragments, offering faster and more precise results. It is commonly used for medium to small-scale applications and is highly automated for efficient sequencing.</p></p>
