<p><p>Courier Tape is used to seal packages for courier and shipping purposes. The market for this type of tape can be categorized into single-sided adhesive tapes and double-sided adhesive tapes. Single-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on one side, and they are used to seal packages by sticking the tape onto the surface. Double-sided adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, and they are used to join or bond two surfaces together. Both types of tapes play a crucial role in providing secure and reliable packaging solutions for various courier and shipping needs.</p></p>
