<p><p>The industrial machine vision market comprises two main types: PC-based and Smart Camera-based systems. PC-based systems involve the use of a computer to process and analyze images captured by cameras. They offer high processing power and flexibility, making them suitable for complex applications. On the other hand, Smart Camera-based systems integrate image capture, processing, and analysis functions within a single device. They are compact, cost-effective, and easier to deploy, making them ideal for simpler industrial vision applications.</p></p>
