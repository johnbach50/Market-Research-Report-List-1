<p><p>Aircraft fuel market consists of two main types - Avgas and Jet Fuel. Avgas is specially designed for piston-engine aircraft and has a higher octane rating to prevent engine knocking. Jet Fuel, on the other hand, is used in turbine-engine aircraft and has a lower freezing point to accommodate high-altitude flights. Both fuel types are crucial for the aviation industry, with Avgas catering to smaller, general aviation aircraft and Jet Fuel serving commercial airlines and military aircraft. Both markets are subject to fluctuations in crude oil prices and global demand for air travel.</p></p>
