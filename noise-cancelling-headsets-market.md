The result is a significantly improved audio experience, particularly in noisy environments such as airplanes, offices, or public transportation.</p><p>The global noise-cancelling headsets market has witnessed steady growth in recent years and is expected to continue this trend in the coming years. <p><p>Noise-cancelling headsets are available in three market types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear headphones. Over-ear headphones are larger and cover the entire ear, providing excellent noise isolation. On-ear headphones are smaller and rest on the outer ear, offering decent noise reduction while being more portable. In-ear headphones go directly into the ear canal and offer the most compact design. They are ideal for travel and everyday use but may not provide the same level of noise cancellation as the other two types.</p></p>
