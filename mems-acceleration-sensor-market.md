<p><strong>The MEMS Acceleration Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive MEMS Acceleration Sensor</li><li>Thermal MEMS Acceleration Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS acceleration sensor market comprises two main types: capacitive MEMS acceleration sensors and thermal MEMS acceleration sensors. Capacitive MEMS acceleration sensors utilize changes in capacitance to measure acceleration. They are highly sensitive, accurate, and suitable for various applications. On the other hand, thermal MEMS acceleration sensors leverage changes in temperature to measure acceleration. They are known for their low power consumption, compact size, and ability to detect both static and dynamic acceleration. Both types serve different purposes in the market, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
