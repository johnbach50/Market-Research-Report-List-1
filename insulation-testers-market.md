<p><p>Insulation testers are devices used to measure the effectiveness of insulation materials in electrical systems. There are two main types: low voltage insulation testers and high voltage insulation testers. Low voltage insulation testers are designed for measuring insulation resistance in low voltage systems, typically up to 1000 volts. High voltage insulation testers, on the other hand, are used for testing insulation in high voltage systems, where voltages can reach several hundred or even thousands of volts. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety and efficiency of electrical installations.</p></p>
