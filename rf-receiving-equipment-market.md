<p><p>RF receiving equipment refers to devices used for receiving radio frequency signals. There are various types available in the market. Crystal RF receiving equipment employs a crystal oscillator to receive signals at specific frequencies. Tuned RF receiving equipment uses a tuning circuit to adjust the frequency being received. Superheterodyne RF receiving equipment converts signals to an intermediate frequency for better reception. Super-regenerative RF receiving equipment uses an oscillator that turns on and off rapidly to amplify weak signals. Other types include direct-conversion and digital RF receiving equipment.</p></p>
