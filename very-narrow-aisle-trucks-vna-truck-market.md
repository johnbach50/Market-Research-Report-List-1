<p><p>The Very Narrow Aisle Truck (VNA Truck) market includes two main types of trucks: Reach Trucks and Turret Trucks. Reach Trucks are designed to operate in narrow aisles and have extended reach capabilities to access high storage areas. They have a forward-facing operator compartment and use outriggers for stability. Turret Trucks, on the other hand, have a rotating mast that enables them to operate in aisles as narrow as 1.6 meters. They can rotate the load 180 degrees and place it in any direction, maximizing storage density and efficiency.</p></p>
