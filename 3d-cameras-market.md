<p><p>The 3D Cameras market encompasses three different types: stereo vision, time of flight, and structured light. Stereo vision technology uses two or more cameras to capture images from different angles, enabling depth perception. Time of flight cameras measure the time it takes for emitted light to return, allowing for accurate distance measurements. Structured light cameras project patterns onto objects and use the distortions in the patterns to calculate depth. These technologies are widely used in various applications, such as robotics, gaming, surveillance, and automotive, to enable accurate 3D imaging and sensing capabilities.</p></p>
