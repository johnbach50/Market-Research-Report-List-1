<p><p>ECG Telemeters, also known as EKG telemeters, are portable devices used to record and transmit electrocardiogram (ECG) data wirelessly. These telemeters are designed to monitor patients' heart activity remotely, enabling healthcare professionals to assess the heart's condition and diagnose various cardiac conditions. ECG telemeters typically consist of a device that is attached to the patient's chest, which records the electrical activity of the heart, and a transmitter that sends this data to a monitoring system or healthcare provider via wireless communication.</p><p>The ECG Telemeters Market is experiencing significant growth due to several factors. The increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, along with the need for remote patient monitoring and early detection of cardiac abnormalities, is driving the demand for ECG telemeters. The rising geriatric population, who are more susceptible to cardiovascular diseases, is also fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology have led to the development of innovative ECG telemeters with improved accuracy, reliability, and ease of use. For instance, the introduction of wearable ECG telemeters, such as wristbands and patches, has gained traction in recent years. These wearable devices offer continuous monitoring and real-time transmission of ECG data, enhancing patient convenience and aiding in the early diagnosis of heart conditions.</p><p>Additionally, the COVID-19 pandemic has further propelled the ECG Telemeters Market. With the increasing demand for remote healthcare services, ECG telemeters have played a crucial role in monitoring COVID-19 patients' cardiac health remotely.</p><p>In conclusion, the ECG Telemeters Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 8% during the forecast period. Factors such as the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, technological advancements, and the need for remote patient monitoring are driving market growth.
