<p><p>Composable Infrastructure is a software-defined infrastructure that enables organizations to treat their IT resources as fluid pools of compute, storage, and networking that can be dynamically distributed to meet the needs of different applications. This flexibility and adaptability of composable infrastructure make it a popular choice for modern data centers and cloud environments.</p><p>The Composable Infrastructure Market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
