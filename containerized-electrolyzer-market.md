<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer</li><li>PEM Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Containerized electrolyzers are a type of equipment used to produce hydrogen gas through the process of electrolysis. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use a solution of potassium hydroxide as an electrolyte, while PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers use a solid polymer membrane to separate the electrodes. Both types are housed in containers for ease of use and transportation. Traditional alkaline electrolyzer market is well-established, whereas the PEM electrolyzer market is relatively new but growing due to its higher efficiency and flexibility in operations.</p></p>
