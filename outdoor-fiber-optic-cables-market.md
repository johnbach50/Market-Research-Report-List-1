<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optic Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The outdoor fiber optic cables market can be categorized into two types: single-mode fiber optic cables and multi-mode fiber optic cables. Single-mode fiber optic cables have a narrower core diameter and are used for long-distance communication. They offer higher transmission speeds and less signal loss. On the other hand, multi-mode fiber optic cables have a larger core diameter and are suitable for short-distance communication. They are cost-effective and commonly used in LAN networks. Both types play a crucial role in providing reliable and efficient outdoor communication infrastructure.</p></p>
