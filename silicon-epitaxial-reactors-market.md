MOCVD is a process that involves the deposition of thin films using chemical reactions, whereas MBE uses high-energy beams to deposit thin films. These technologies are primarily used for manufacturing electronic devices. The "Others" segment encompasses various alternative methods employed for silicon epitaxial deposition.
