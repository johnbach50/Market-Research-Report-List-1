<p><strong>The Coal Boring Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transverse Coal Boring Machine</li><li>Longitudinal Coal Boring Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coal boring machine market consists of two main types: transverse coal boring machines and longitudinal coal boring machines. A transverse coal boring machine is designed to bore tunnels horizontally, cutting through the coal seams in a perpendicular direction. On the other hand, a longitudinal coal boring machine is used to create vertical tunnels, drilling downwards through the coal layers. These machines are important in the coal mining industry as they facilitate the extraction of coal resources efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
