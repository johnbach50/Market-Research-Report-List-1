<p><ul><li>Kaolinite</li><li>Smectite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nanoclay market is segmented based on the types of minerals present, with two main categories being Kaolinite and Smectite. Kaolinite nanoclay is known for its high aspect ratio and excellent reinforcement properties in polymers, making it ideal for applications in packaging, construction, and automotive industries. On the other hand, Smectite nanoclay is valued for its high surface area and ability to enhance barrier properties in various materials, finding use in the pharmaceutical, cosmetics, and environmental sectors. Both types offer unique properties that cater to different market needs.</p></p>
