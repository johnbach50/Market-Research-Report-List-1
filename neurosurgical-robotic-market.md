<p><p>The neurosurgical robotic market can be classified into two types: portable and fixed. Portable neurosurgical robots are designed to be mobile and can be easily moved between different operating rooms. They offer flexibility and convenience to healthcare professionals by allowing them to perform neurosurgery in different locations. On the other hand, fixed neurosurgical robots are permanently installed in a specific operating room. They provide a stable and precise platform for performing complex neurosurgical procedures, ensuring accuracy and reducing the risk of errors. Both types of robots cater to different needs and preferences of neurosurgeons and hospitals.</p></p>
