<p><p>Door dampers are devices used to control the closing speed and force of doors, preventing them from slamming shut. There are two main types of door dampers: surface applied door dampers and concealed door dampers. Surface applied dampers are visible on the surface of the door, attached to hinges, and are easily installed. On the other hand, concealed dampers are hidden within door frames, offering a more aesthetic appearance. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring controlled and silent door closure, but differ in terms of visibility and installation process.</p></p>
