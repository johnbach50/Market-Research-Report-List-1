<p><p>The MMA (Manual Metal Arc) Welder market can be categorized into two types based on power supply: AC power and DC power. AC power welders are widely used for welding materials like steel and cast iron, as the alternating current allows for better penetration and heat control. DC power welders, on the other hand, are preferred for welding stainless steel, aluminum, and other non-ferrous metals, as the direct current provides stable arc control and prevents electrode sticking. These two types cater to different welding requirements and offer varying benefits in terms of weld quality and heat management.</p></p>
