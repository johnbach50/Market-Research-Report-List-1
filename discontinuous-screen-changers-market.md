<p><p>In the market, Discontinuous Screen Changers are available in two main types: Manual and Hydraulic. </p><p>Manual Discontinuous Screen Changers require operators to manually change the screens when they become clogged or dirty. This type is less expensive but requires more labor and downtime. </p><p>Hydraulic Discontinuous Screen Changers use a hydraulic system to automatically change the screens, reducing the need for manual intervention and increasing efficiency. This type is more expensive but offers higher productivity and less downtime.</p></p>
