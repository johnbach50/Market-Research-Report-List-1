Moreover, stringent regulations and guidelines pertaining to indoor air quality have also been driving the demand for duct mounted access doors.</p><p>Additionally, the market growth can be attributed to the rising construction activities in both residential and commercial sectors, which has led to the installation of more HVAC systems and subsequently increased the demand for access doors.
