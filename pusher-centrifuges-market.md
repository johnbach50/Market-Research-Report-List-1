<p><p>Pusher centrifuges are industrial machines used for solid-liquid separation in various industries. There are two market types of pusher centrifuges: single-stage centrifuge and multi-stage centrifuge. </p><p>A single-stage centrifuge consists of a rotating drum that pushes the solids towards the end using centrifugal force, while the liquid is drained out. It is suitable for applications where the solid content is high and the liquid phase needs to be drained quickly.</p><p>In contrast, a multi-stage centrifuge consists of multiple rotating drums arranged in series. The separated solids from the previous stage are further dewatered in subsequent stages, resulting in a higher degree of solid-liquid separation. This type of centrifuge is ideal for applications that require an even higher level of solid separation efficiency.</p></p>
