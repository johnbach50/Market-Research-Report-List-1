<p><p>The small business paper shredders market can be categorized into three types: cross cut, strip cut, and others. Cross-cut shredders use two rotating blades to cut paper in both vertical and horizontal directions, resulting in small confetti-like pieces. Strip-cut shredders shred paper into long, thin strips. Other types include micro-cut shredders, which produce tiny particles, and diamond-cut shredders, which create diamond-shaped pieces. Each type offers varying levels of security and efficiency, allowing small businesses to choose the one that best suits their needs for disposing of confidential documents.</p></p>
