<p><p>Freeze drying technology is used to remove moisture from products while preserving their quality and properties. Tray-style freeze drying involves placing products on trays and freezing them. Manifold freeze drying uses a manifold system to freeze products. Rotary freeze drying, also known as shell freeze drying, involves rotating the product and subjecting it to low pressure and temperature. These technologies offer various advantages such as extended shelf life, minimal loss of nutrients, and improved stability, making them desirable for industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology.</p></p>
