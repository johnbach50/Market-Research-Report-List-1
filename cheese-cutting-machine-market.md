<p><p>The cheese cutting machine market can be categorized into two major types: ultrasonics and classical. Ultrasonic cheese cutting machines use high-frequency sound waves to precisely slice the cheese without compressing or deforming it, ensuring a consistent and neat cut. This technology is advanced and offers high efficiency. On the other hand, classical cheese cutting machines use traditional cutting methods like knives or wires. These machines have been in use for a long time and are reliable for their simplicity and effectiveness in cutting various types of cheese.</p></p>
