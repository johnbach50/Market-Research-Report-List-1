<p><strong>The Machine Vision Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision cameras are essential components in industrial automation and robotic systems. There are two main types: CCD (charge-coupled device) cameras and CMOS (complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras use a specialized image sensor to capture high-quality images with low noise levels, making them ideal for applications that require precise image analysis. On the other hand, CMOS cameras use a different image sensor technology, offering faster data readout and lower power consumption. CMOS cameras are more cost-effective and suitable for applications where high-speed image acquisition is crucial. Both types play significant roles in various industries.</p></p>
