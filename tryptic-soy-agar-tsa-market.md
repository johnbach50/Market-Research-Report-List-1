<p><p>Tryptic Soy Agar (TSA) is a type of solid medium used for the cultivation of bacteria, yeasts, and molds. TSA provides a nutritious and supportive environment for the growth and proliferation of a wide range of microorganisms. It is commonly used in clinical, pharmaceutical, and food microbiology laboratories for various purposes such as isolation, enumeration, and identification of microorganisms.</p><p>The Tryptic Soy Agar TSA market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
