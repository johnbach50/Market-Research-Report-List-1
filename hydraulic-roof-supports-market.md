<p><p>Hydraulic roof supports refer to equipment used in underground mining operations to provide support to the roof. There are different types of hydraulic roof supports available in the market. Chock support refers to a support system where the top and bottom halves of the support are separate and can be independently controlled. Shield support involves a single hydraulic cylinder pushing against the roof. Chock shield support combines the features of chock support and shield support, providing adjustable roof support with added stability.</p></p>
