These relays are available in two types: Voltage Measurement and Current Measurement. </p><p>Voltage Measurement Monitoring Relays are designed to measure and monitor the voltage levels in electrical systems. They provide accurate readings and help in detecting any anomalies or fluctuations in the voltage supply, ensuring the safety and proper functioning of the system.</p><p>On the other hand, Current Measurement Monitoring Relays are used to measure and monitor the electrical current flowing through the system. These relays help in identifying overcurrent or undercurrent situations, preventing damages and ensuring efficient operation of the electrical equipment.</p></p>
