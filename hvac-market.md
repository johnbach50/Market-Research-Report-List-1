<p><p>HVAC market types can be categorized into two main systems: Direct Expansion Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. Direct Expansion Systems, also known as DX systems, use individual units to cool or heat specific areas directly. These systems are commonly used in small residential or commercial spaces. On the other hand, Central Air Conditioning Systems use a centralized unit to distribute conditioned air throughout a building via ductwork. These systems are more commonly found in larger commercial or industrial buildings, providing efficient cooling or heating to multiple areas simultaneously.</p></p>
