<p><p>Novel Drug Delivery Systems refer to advanced methods of transporting medications to specific targets within the body. Liposomes are tiny vesicles that can encapsulate drugs and deliver them to target sites. PEGylated Proteins & Polypeptides involve modifying proteins with polyethylene glycol to enhance their stability and drug-carrying ability. Polymer Nanoparticles employ biocompatible polymers to encapsulate drugs for targeted delivery. Protein-drug Conjugates involve attaching drugs to proteins to increase their specificity and effectiveness. The "Others" category encompasses various innovative drug delivery systems, such as viral vectors, nanoparticles, and implantable devices.</p></p>
