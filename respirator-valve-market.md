Proportional valves enable precise flow control by adjusting the valve opening based on the input signal. Other types of valves in this market include ball valves, butterfly valves, and check valves, which offer different functionalities for respiratory equipment. These valves play a crucial role in regulating the air supply and ventilation in respiratory devices.</p></p>
