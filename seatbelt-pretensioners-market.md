<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Seatbelt Pretensioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lap Pretensioners</li><li>Buckle Pretensioners</li><li>Retractor Pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Seatbelt pretensioners are devices used in vehicles to tighten seatbelts during a collision, ensuring maximum safety for occupants. Lap pretensioners are located near the anchor point of the lap belt and secure it tightly across the occupant's lap. Buckle pretensioners are located in the seatbelt buckle and retract the slack from the lap belt. Retractor pretensioners are built into the seatbelt retractor and retract the entire length of the seatbelt. Each pretensioner type plays a crucial role in enhancing seatbelt effectiveness and minimizing occupant movement during a crash.</p></p>
