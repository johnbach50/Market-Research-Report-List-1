<p><p>Hearing Protection Equipment (HPE) refers to devices utilized to prevent or reduce noise-induced hearing loss. There are two common types of HPE available in the market - Earplugs and Earmuffs. Earplugs are small devices that are inserted into the ear canal to block or absorb sound waves, offering a portable and discreet solution. On the other hand, earmuffs are larger devices that cover the entire ear, providing a physical barrier against noise. Both earplugs and earmuffs are designed to protect the wearer's hearing by reducing the impact of noise exposure.</p></p>
